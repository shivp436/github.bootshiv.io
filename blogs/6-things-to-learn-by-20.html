6 Things You Must Learn By 20

Nov 14, 2020 · 3 min read

Okay, without much beating behind the bush. Let's get straight into it.

It’s Okay To Be Uncertain Sometimes

Sometimes, you cannot see the path ahead and so you start feeling lost. You start doubting your own capability. You ask yourself “Am I on the right path?”

You may not get an immediate answer, but you will surely get an answer.

And it’s okay if the answer is a “NO”, you have got enough time to change your path. It’s okay if the answer is a “YES” and yet you aren’t sure of the answer, maybe the path is just foggy now.

Sometimes a ‘maybe’ instead of a ‘must be’ is okay.

And maybe this is the time to stop waiting for certainty and take a leap of faith, maybe this is the time to follow your intuition and not the ratio.

May be it’s the time to Believe In Yourself And Only Yourself.

Be Kind To Yourself And Creatures Around You

When you start your day with kindness and gratitude , you are all set to be happy.

Kindness costs you no penny.

Be grateful for every moment you have ever lived, everything you have ever experienced and everyone you have ever met in your whole life.

Be grateful about the past, present and the upcoming future.

Waking up each morning is a blessing in itself, and you must be grateful for everything that follows along.

When You Are In Gratitude, You Can Be Nowhere Else, and there can be no better place to be than gratitude.

Manage Your Money

It’s said that money cannot buy you happiness, but it can surely buy you the resources you need to live a happy life if you use it in the right way.

Start saving money, invest it on yourself if not in some funds.

At least 30% of the money you get/earn should be invested on yourself.

As Dave Ramsey said,

We buy things we don’t want want, to impress people we don’t like

So yeah, just stop buying dumb shit.

Make Hobbies

Hobbies are one of the things that make your life worth living. They rebuild your brain’s peace and build you for the shit that’s coming ahead. And as long as you love to do it, you do not need to worry about the rest of the world validating it.

Have at least one hobby, which is not just cool to show-off but also loving to do.

Start To Cut Out Negativity

We all have negative energy around us, in the form of people and things and some events. While cutting it off completely may not be possible, try to avoid it as much as you can.

Limiting the people you have around yourself is one of the best ways to start with.

Instead of scrolling through pages of social media, practice meditation or do some workout or whatever gives you a good return of interest.

Learn How To Make Use Of Social Platforms

Social platforms are not just for consuming stuff, they are an obvious source of one’s audience if you know the right stuff and have the required skill.

And in this generation, blaming that Instagram spreads negativity makes no sense, you have to manage how you consume the content available.

And when it comes to uploading something on these platforms, if you can, spread positivity, and if you cannot, then better don’t post anything.

Thank you for reading.
I hope you got something valuable from here.
