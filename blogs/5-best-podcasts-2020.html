The 5 Best Podcasts Of 2020

Sep 14, 2020 · 2 min read

On Purpose With Jay Shetty

In a world full of chaos and negativity (Especially this fu*ked up 2020), this podcast by Jay Shetty, a former monk, will help you in organizing your life and to live a life of purpose

Episode 11 Of On Purpose With retired United States Navy SEAL and the toughest man on Earth David Goggins is my personal favorite.

7 Good Minutes

If you are someone who is always on the go and do not have time for those hour long episodes, then this podcast by Clyde Lee Dennis is the perfect match for you. It is also pretty surprising to know that he covers everything from Spiritual Meditations to Business Insider Tips, all in just seven minutes a day.

The GaryVee Audio Experience

Hosted by Gary Vaynerchuk, is the best ever podcast for young business brains who want to focus on their real audience and not just the algorithms and numbers. He always talks about how to stay in contact with your audience and also a lot about positivity.

P.S. If you listen to him daily, you may, within a week, stop judging yourself and start doing more

The Bible Meditation Podcast

After a long-busy day, we surely need something to slow us down and take the weight off our shoulders. This is the podcast that never fails to do so. The Bible Meditation Podcast hosted by Nikki Rach brings a perfect combination of her soothing voice and the melodious background music with The Bible Verses.

P.S. You can also use this as sleep music
